How it works

Three clicks to a map.

No copy-pasting. No searching. PlaceSnap reads the video for you.

01
Open any travel video
Works on YouTube, Instagram posts and reels, and TikTok videos. Any language, any destination.
02
Click PlaceSnap
A panel slides open with every location extracted by AI — cities, beaches, landmarks, viewpoints, restaurants.
03
Edit, then save
Delete wrong pins, rename places, add missing ones. Then export to Google Maps or save your trip list as HTML.
04
Every pin links back
Your saved maps remember the source video. Click any pin months later and go straight back to the content that inspired it.
Features

Built for real trip planning.

Not just a list of place names — a proper tool for travelers who plan from content.

🤖
AI-powered extraction
Uses Claude (Anthropic) to read transcripts, captions and descriptions — finds specific places, not just country names.
✏️
Edit before saving
Delete wrong pins, rename vague ones, add places manually. You control what ends up in your map.
🗺
Export to Google Maps
One click opens all locations in Google Maps. Export as KML to import into Google My Maps with all pins at once.
🎬
Source video in every pin
Every saved pin links back to the video it came from. Plan your trip months later and never lose the context.
💾
Save as HTML
Export a beautiful offline-ready location page you can share, keep, or use without internet.
🔒
Your data stays yours
Your API key is stored locally on your device. PlaceSnap never collects, stores or shares your data.
